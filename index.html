📋 HOW TO USE

1

Download & Extract

Download the ZIP file and extract it anywhere on your PC.

2

Run SETUP.bat

Double-click SETUP.bat — it installs the tool and creates a Desktop shortcut automatically.

3

Prepare Your Phone

Connect phone via USB. Enable USB Debugging in Developer Options. Tap ALLOW on your phone screen.

4

Open MEmu

Launch MEmu emulator on your PC and wait for Android to fully load.

5

Run & Backup!

Open the tool → Click Scan Devices → Click Start WhatsApp Backup → Done!
